Return Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

1. Eligibility for Returns

You may return eligible items within 30 days of delivery, provided they meet the following conditions:

  • Items are unused, unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Items are not custom-made, personalized, or marked as final sale.
  • You have proof of purchase (e.g., order confirmation email or receipt).

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or refunded unless defective:

  • Custom or personalized items.
  • Underwear, swimwear, or other items deemed non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
  • Items marked as “final sale” at the time of purchase.

3. Return Process

  1. Contact us at support@snakewear.com within 30 days of delivery to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Include your order number and reason for return.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, including all tags and accessories.
  3. Include the RMA number in your return package.
  4. Ship the package to the return address provided in your RMA confirmation using a trackable shipping method.

Note: You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error in your order.

4. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • We will notify you via email of approval or rejection of your refund.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days. Shipping fees, taxes, and duties are non-refundable.
  • Refunds may take additional time to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider.

5. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for items of equal or lesser value, subject to availability. To request an exchange:

  • Follow the return process above and indicate your preferred replacement item.
  • If the replacement item is less expensive, we will refund the difference. If more expensive, you will be charged the difference.

6. Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect item:

  • Contact us at support@snakewear.com within 7 days of delivery with photos of the issue.
  • We will provide a prepaid return shipping label and either replace the item or issue a full refund, including shipping costs.

7. International Returns

International customers may return eligible items within 30 days, but you are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs duties or taxes. We recommend using a trackable shipping method. Refunds will be issued in USD, and currency conversion fees may apply.
